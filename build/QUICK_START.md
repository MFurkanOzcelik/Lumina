## ğŸ†˜ Troubleshooting

**"I don't have Python"**
- No problem! Just make sure your images are:
  - Square (same width and height)
  - At least 512x512 pixels
  - PNG format with transparency
- Then rename them to `icon.png` and `document-icon.png`

**"Icons look squished"**
- Your source images aren't square
- Use online tool: https://www.iloveimg.com/crop-image
- Select 1:1 aspect ratio
- Add padding (don't stretch)

**"Icons don't update after building"**
- Delete old executable
- Clear Windows icon cache: `ie4uinit.exe -show`
- Rebuild
